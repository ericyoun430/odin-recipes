Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Seasonings
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Break the chicken down into the thighs, breasts, wings, and legs
  2. Have the chicken marinate in buttermilk that has seasonings within it
  3. Let it sit for a couple hours
  4. Cover it in flour
  5. Fry until golden and crispy
